WHAT HAPPENS WHEN YOU
ENGAGE WITH US?
+ PLAN AND INTEGRATE
We examine your robotic application and operation requirements, before customizing and installing a proposed solution on a trial system. A full report accompanies the resulting integration recommendation.

A vital element of the handover of the new system concerns the transfer of detailed knowledge to your team on how to operate and customize your deployment as your robotic applications evolve.
+ DEPLOY
The system is rolled out and configured for optimum performance. At this stage, your MoveIt! Advantage subscription begins, making ongoing support easier and more cost-effective.

More than ever, having a team that can provide quick software support is crucial to avoiding project delays or blockers. PickNik support guarantees a minimum of 10 hours/month support for those critical tasks.
+ UPGRADE
As your application needs evolve, we collaborate closely with your team to optimise your installation on an ongoing basis, taking advantages of any improvements to enhance the efficiency.
