OUR TEAM
PickNik has over 23 years of experience in the field of robotics and automation and the team is rooted in a deep academic background of robotics theory.

Dave Coleman, PhD
Co-Founder, Roboticist
B.S. Mechanical Engineering Georgia Tech, Masters and PhD Computer Science CU Boulder, has 11 years of experience working in the field of robotics automation, including the motion planning software MoveIt! along with the rest of ROS. Dave has been a leader in the open source community and has a PhD in robotic motion planning. His insights into the needs of a robot-agnostic platform that works for different morphologies, different theoretical approaches, and different end-user technical needs have given him a well-rounded understanding of the needs of powerful robotic software.

Andy McEvoy, PhD
Co-Founder, Roboticist
M.S. Aerospace Engineering from Texas A&M focusing on dynamics and controls and a PhD in Computer Science from CU Boulder. Andy has worked in the aerospace sector on spaceflight and military grade hardware. Since then he has worked for MDA helping to develop the next generation of robotic arms for on-orbit assembly and servicing. Andy has a wide breadth of experience that extends from the initial mechanical design and analysis to the final software deployment of robotic systems.

Mike Lautman
Roboticist
Mike holds a BS in computer science and electrical engineering as well as a MS in Robotics all from the University of Pennsylvania. While still a full-time student, Mike founded Nebu, a Machine Learning and Data Science consultancy that he grew from 0 to 10 engineers. One of the projects that Mike is especially proud of involved collaborating with Thorn, Ashton Kutcher's nonprofit, to build an ETL pipeline that feeds millions of escort advertisements from the web into a singular database to be used by law-enforcement in building cases against child-traffickers. After Nebu, Mike joined up with KindredAi, an AI and Robotics startup where he took on leadership of their motion planning efforts on a number of both internal and external products.

Rob Coleman
Business Manager
Rob has lived and worked on 4 continents and has developed a talent for seeing the big picture, in both startups and large companies. As one of PickNik's business consultants he strives to move its vision forward by digging into the details and making improvements wherever he can.

Mike Hurowitz
Advisor
Michael is a serial entrepreneur and startup advisor. Michael's expertise is in research commercialization, bringing new technologies from the lab to market. He has delivered over $24B of space missions, $7B of commercial aircraft, and 127 new products developed working with 35 Fortune 500 companies and almost 60 startups, 14 federal agencies, and 16 university research groups.

Jeff Rose
Advisor
Jeff is a Co-founder and CEO of ThinkTopic, a Boulder based AI company. Over the last 3 years ThinkTopic has worked with a wide range of organizations, from the Asian Development Bank to Conde Nast and Digital Globe. Before starting ThinkTopic Jeff was one of the first research engineers at (Google) DeepMind, where he collaborated with neuroscientists to develop novel machine learning algorithms.
