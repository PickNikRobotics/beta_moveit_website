PickNik combines world-class robotics expertise and state of the art open source robotics frameworks to save you time and money. We offer flexible support and development to ensure your project's success. From ROS integration to complex inverse kinematics and motion planning, we have you covered.

Solve your unique
robotic challenges

We strongly believe in the benefits of open source, such as avoiding vendor lock in and allowing your engineers to modify any software as they see fit. PickNik is the lead maintainer of the MoveIt Motion Planning Framework. In addition, we contribute to and advocate the Robotic Operating System (ROS) including the following components:

  • ROS Control and Real-time Systems
  • Open Motion Planning Library (OMPL)
  • ROS Navigation Stack
  • Gazebo Simulation
