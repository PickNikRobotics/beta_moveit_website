We strongly believe in the benefits of open source; PickNik is the lead maintainer of MoveIt and is very active in the ROS community. By leveraging open standards and best practices we are compatible with almost any kind of hardware. We are experienced in the integration of robotic hardware including Universal Robotics (UR3/UR5/UR10), Franka Emika (Panda), Kuka, ABB, Kinova, and many custom platforms.
